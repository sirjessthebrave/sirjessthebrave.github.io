About
Jessica Bell

Jessica is a web developer for the Washington Post and instructor at General Assembly. She holds a degree in International Relations from San Fransisco State and studied abroad at Cardiff University and the University of Burgundy. She has served on the leadership teams for DC Tech Meetup, DCFemTech, DC ACM, and is an active member of Women Who Code DC . She also hosts a podcast called DC Tech Stories about local tech workers. She enjoyes creating side projecs such as DC Tech Stories, a podcast about tech workers in the DMV, her current project is Global Tech Stories!

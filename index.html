Jessica Ryan Bell

Employment

Jessica is a font end developer at RepEquity a digital agency focusing on reputation management, on-line branding, and advocacy. She works with HTML, CSS, SASS, Drupal, JavaScript, JQuery, PHP, and Angular. Her projects range from one page branding sites, to larger scale digital advocacy platforms. She assists the team of developers and designers to produce high quality websites with an eye for well written code and good SEO practices. She produces events at RepEquity as well, including a Hackathon, and 'The Developer's Journey'; both events boasting equal gender representation! Jessica also coined Flanimal Friday, in which colleagues don their hippest flannel tops over their favorite animal t-shirt - talk about innovation!

She also works at General Assembly at a teaching assistant for the Front End Web Development (FEWD) course. As a graduate of the FEWD course, Jessica enjoys helping new developers learn HTML, CSS, Git/GitHub, and JavaScript/JQuery. She aids students with homework and projects, holds office hours to reiterate lessons, and provides extra support during her student's course.

Previously Jessica worked as Digital Media Coordinator for the American Friends of the Alexander von Humboldt Foundation managing the organizations digital presence, collaborating with the Research in Germany team and representing the Alexander von Humboldt Foundation at academic conferences and events.
